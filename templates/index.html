Introduction:
Training Includes covering theory of programming. A practical approach is designed for candidates from every background considering real time scenarios. At the end of everyday a handout is given consisting of interesting facts and problems which helps the candidate to prepare for the next day session.
Course will be highly interactive, includes live coding sessions and reviewing performance of the candidate. Group discussion will be held amongst the team headed by a trainee suggesting the areas of improvement.
Objective of the workshop:
Help and improve the programming knowledge and experience in shortest time possible. Develop a framework essential for a candidate to prospect him/herself in the areas of software development and maintenance. Timeline:
The Course is determined for 3 days. Here is the outline of the workshop. Inception of Programming [Day 1 ]:
1. Recording the data. Inscription on stones.
2. Why Storage. Automation of storage example.
3. Implement using registers and transistors.
4. First Programming Language. Machine or Binary to current day Programming.
5. Why Branches and why there is need of many languages.
6. Branches of programming.
7. Q&A

Programming paradigms [ Day 1 ]:
1. Structure of Elements.
2. Imperative, declarative, functional, object-oriented, procedural, logic and symbolic.
3. Handling Data using Programming paradigms.
4. Synthesis of New Data? Possibility?
5. Communication using Data.
6. Q&A

SDLC [Day 2]:
1. Introduction to process.
2. Waterfall vs Agile.
3. UML: A real noteworthy proposition of any solution.
4. Adaptability and other clauses.
5. Q&A

Applications and their Development [Day 2]:
1. Programming Basics.
2. “Need to know” ‘s of programming.
3. Principles, syntax, standards of programming. [Depends on the Language of choice.]
4. Optimization Techniques.
5. Q&A
Live coding session from scratch and Other Business Intuitive discussions.[ Day 3 ]
1. Live coding session
2. Mock test
3. Current Day happening and overview of Updates [News Update in Programming world]
4. Group Discussions.
